01. history

a bit about our past

Our restaurant was established in May, 1987. Since then, it has grown to more than 30 restaurants worldwide and plans to continue to expand, that is not the end of the story... We have a passion for the French way of life and want to share it with everyone, no matter where they come from, just delicious food for everyone.

We create valuable experiences with our customers when using our restaurant services. As long as you want to order food at our restaurant, we will guarantee you the best and most useful services.

02. About

a bit about us

Our family has been in the restaurant business for a very long time. Today, we can boast of our reputation as a famous French restaurant in our area. We are known for our excellent authentic cuisine, professional chefs and dedicated staff. Choose any dish from the menu that can help you experience the true taste of France.

Taking pride in providing the same original family recipes and products throughout the area no matter where you are.

03. Our Passion

a bit Our Passion

We want to share our passion for great food and great service. We cook our dishes from the freshest ingredients. We specialize in homemade French food, sauces, soups, desserts and more in a warm friendly atmosphere.

We cater events of any sizes to ensure a memorable dining experience through a high quality and elite service possible.
